Delete your SuperDriver account

If you would like to delete your SuperDriver account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@superdriver.com
  2. Use the subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email or phone number used to create your account

What data will be deleted

  • User profile information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Order history
  • Chat messages

Data retention

Some transaction records may be kept for legal and financial requirements.

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days.
